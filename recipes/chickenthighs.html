Grilled Chicken Thighs for Cutting Recipe

Grilled chicken thighs I make almost every day... I recommend changing up the seasonings so you don't get too tired of eating the same thing every day....

You could also substitute the cooking spray with butter/oil for more fat and better taste but at the cost of more calories

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Any seasoning
  • Non-stick 0 calorie cooking spray

Steps

  1. Wash chicken and pat dry
  2. Heat pan up
  3. Add dry seasonings to chicken
  4. Put chicken on pan, medium heat
  5. Heat each side for 4-5 minutes each
  6. Let rest for another 3-5 minutes afterwards
  7. Make sure the chicken isn't raw inside afterwards and enjoy
