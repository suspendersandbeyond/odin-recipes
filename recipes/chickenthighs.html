Grilled Chicken Thighs Cutting Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Any seasoning to your desire that you should mix up so you don't get tired of eating the same thing every day
  • Non-stick 0 calorie cooking spray

Steps

  1. Wash chicken and pat dry
  2. Heat pan up
  3. Add dry seasonings to chicken
  4. Put chicken on pan, medium heat
  5. Heat each side for 4-5 minutes each
  6. Let rest for another 3-5 minutes afterwards
  7. Make sure the chicken isn't raw inside afterwards and enjoy
