Low Calorie Baked Brussel Sprouts

Basic baked brussel sprouts

Ingredients

  • Brussel sprouts
  • Any seasonings
  • Aluminum foil and tray
  • Non-stick 0 calorie spray

Steps

  1. Wash brussels sprouts if not prewashed
  2. Cut stems off brussel sprouts, then slice in half vertically
  3. Place on tray lined with alumninum foil
  4. Spray with nonstick 0 calorie spray
  5. Season with anything you want
  6. Bake 300 degrees F for 10 minutes
