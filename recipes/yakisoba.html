Yakisoba Recipe

A Yakisoba recipe I finangled together from reading "How to Grill Our Love" the manga and watching Rie's Tasty youtube video on yakisoba

Ingredients

  • Grated ginger
  • Sliced or minced garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Tonkotsu Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Thin Pork Slices
  • Sliced Cabbage
  • Yakisoba Noodles

Steps

  1. Add sesame oil to heated pan
  2. Add garlic and ginger to pan
  3. Add pork to pan let it brown a little bit
  4. Add cabbage once pork starts browning and let it break down
  5. Remove pork and cabbage from pan (you will add it back later)
  6. Add more sesame oil to pan, add noodles + water--let the noodles separates/cook a little bit
  7. Add pork and cabbage back to pan with noodles
  8. Add 3 tablespoons of oyster sauce and 1 tablespoon of tonkotsu sauce along with a pinch of salt
  9. Mix
  10. Garnish with pepper and enjoy
